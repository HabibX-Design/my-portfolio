## Project Goal

The store needed to feel trustworthy before asking customers to buy supplements online. The design direction balances premium product presentation with practical ecommerce sections: benefits, usage guidance, bundle options, reviews, and subscription messaging.

## What I Designed

- A polished homepage structure for a health and wellness product line
- Product detail sections for ingredients, outcomes, usage, and FAQs
- Bundle and subscription blocks that make repeat purchases easier to understand
- Mobile-first cards, sticky CTA moments, and clean trust badges

## Conversion Focus

Supplement buyers need clarity. The layout gives them reasons to trust the brand, compare product options, and move toward checkout without feeling lost.

## Result

A Shopify-ready portfolio concept that shows how HabibX Design approaches premium ecommerce pages: clean visuals, structured content, and a direct path to purchase.
