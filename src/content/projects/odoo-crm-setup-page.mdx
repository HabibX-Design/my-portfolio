## Project Goal

The page needed to explain a technical CRM service in plain business language. Instead of overwhelming visitors with software details, the design shows how Odoo can support sales, inventory, invoicing, and follow-up workflows.

## What I Designed

- A service-page structure for Odoo / CRM setup offers
- Sections for pipeline management, automation, inventory, and invoicing
- Simple workflow cards that explain how the system fits daily operations
- CTA areas for consultation requests and setup inquiries

## Conversion Focus

CRM buyers want confidence that the setup will be practical. The page presents the system as a business workflow improvement, not just a software installation.

## Result

A polished CRM setup case study that gives recruiters and clients a clear view of HabibX Design's ability to handle service websites and operational systems.
